Biryani is Just a State of Happiness

A world-renowned Indian dish, biryani takes time and practice
to make but is worth every bit of the effort.
Long-grained rice (like basmati) flavored with fragrant spices such as
saffron and layered with lamb, chicken, fish, or vegetables and a thick gravy are boiled on low flame with covered lid.

Location:

Biryani House,opp.SBI Bank,RajajiNagar,Ananthapur.
